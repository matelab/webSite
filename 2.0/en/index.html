    About us...

    We are young software workers cooperative, we born in 2019 and here is some data about us that could be of your interest:

    • We are passionate about code.
    • Our main foundation is inclusion.
    • People first, then bussines.
    • We are an horizontal company where each one has a vote and every decision is taken as a team.
    • We love open source.
    • Our preferred stacks are PHP and Python(but we code in .Net is needed).
    • We have "No clients Friday" which mean we use that day of the week to grown as a team and we don't talk with clients on Friday.
    • We drink mate all the time.
