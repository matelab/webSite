    About us...

    We are young software workers cooperative created by our interest in programming and self-management. We were born in 2019 and since 2021 we are part of FACTTIC(Argentine Federation of Technology, Innovation and Knowledge Work Cooperatives). Some data about MateLab:

    • We are passionate about code.
    • Our main foundation is inclusion.
    • We are an horizontal company.
    • We work with agile methodologies.
    • We prioritize clear and direct communication.
    • We love open source.
